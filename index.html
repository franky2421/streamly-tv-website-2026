Setup Centre

Choose your device for simple setup steps.

🔥 Firestick / Fire TV
  1. Install your preferred compatible IPTV player.
  2. Open the app and enter the details provided by StreamlyTV Support.
  3. Save and refresh the playlist.
  4. Message support if you need help.
📺 Android TV / Google TV
  1. Open Google Play Store.
  2. Install a compatible player app.
  3. Enter your StreamlyTV details.
  4. Restart the app if channels do not load.
📱 Android Phone / Tablet
  1. Install a compatible player app.
  2. Add your account details.
  3. Allow permissions if requested.
  4. Start watching.
🍎 iPhone / iPad
  1. Install a compatible IPTV player from the App Store.
  2. Add the details from StreamlyTV Support.
  3. Save the profile and refresh.
📺 Samsung / LG Smart TV
  1. Install a compatible TV player app.
  2. Send your MAC address or device ID to support.
  3. Wait for activation confirmation.
  4. Restart the TV app.
💻 Windows / Mac
  1. Install a compatible media/IPTV player.
  2. Add your playlist or login details.
  3. Save and refresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Choose a plan, pay securely, then send your device details to StreamlyTV Support on WhatsApp.

Which plan should I choose?

3 months is good to try, 6 months is best value, and 12 months is best for long-term customers.

How do I renew?

Go to Plans & Pricing, choose your renewal plan, then message support with your payment confirmation.

What devices are supported?

Many Smart TVs, Android TV, Google TV, Firestick, Android phones, iPhone, tablets, Windows and Mac can work with compatible player apps.

What internet speed do I need?

For best results, use a stable internet connection. Higher quality streams need faster and more reliable internet.

How do I contact support?

Use the WhatsApp button on this website to message StreamlyTV Support.

Need help?

Talk to StreamlyTV Support for setup, pricing, renewals and troubleshooting.
